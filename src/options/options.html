Opt-In to Customizations

Mozilla requires Thunderkey to get your permission to store your customizations on your hard drive.
If it's okay to do so, please opt-in by clicking the box below.
If you don't opt-in, only the default keyboard shortcuts will work.
If you opt-in but then change your mind, you can uncheck the box and erase your customizations.

Quick Actions

To change the shortcut for a command, click the "Edit" button and then press the desired keyboard shortcut.

Folder Preferences

  1. Select one of your system's mail folders.
  2. Specify the keyboard shortcut that should result in selecting that folder.
  3. Specify the keyboard shortcut that should move selected messages to that folder.

Favorite Folders for Sent Messages

Select one or more folders per email account where you commonly file your sent messages.
